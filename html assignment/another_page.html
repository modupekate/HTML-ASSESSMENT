WEB DESIGN

If you're designing a website on your own, you will need to wear many hats. The good news is that you probably won't notice. As a solo web designer, you may be part time graphic desighner, writer, HTML author, and information architect, but to you it'll just feel like "making web pages.

There are specialists out there whom you can hire to fill in the skills you don't have. Large-scale websites are almost always created by a team of people, numbering from a handful of hundreds.

In this scenerio, each member of the team focuses on one facet of the site-building process. if that is the case, you may be able to simply adapt your current set of skills and interest to a new medium.
